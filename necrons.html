Necrons Unit List

Army Rule
Reanimation Protocols: Every unit (except “C’tan) may activate this ability to restore d3 wounds to itself every activation. Models must be healed to full before returning any new models.

Select one Military Tradition from below:
Awakened Dynasty
Gain the following:
• Each time a “Royal” unit activates, select 2x friendly units within 6”. Those units may make 1x action in this unit’s activation.
• If a unit has a “Leader” attached to it, restore +1 wounds when it uses Reanimation Protocols.


Destroyer Cult
Gain the following:
• Re-roll charge rolls and +1 AP.
• When a “Destroyer” unit destroys an enemy unit, all “Destroyer” units may take 1x action.
